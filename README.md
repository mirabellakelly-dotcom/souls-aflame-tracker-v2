Souls Aflame Tracker – Church Administration Made Simple
Souls Aflame Tracker is a modern church management application designed to help ministries stay organized, efficient, and intentional in their operations. It provides a simple way to manage member records, track attendance, and handle essential administrative tasks in one place.
Built as a fast and reliable Progressive Web App (PWA), the app delivers a smooth, app-like experience directly from the browser and can be installed on mobile devices. It also supports offline functionality, ensuring that church activities and records remain accessible even without an internet connection.
✨ Features
Member management
Attendance tracking
Clean and simple interface
Works offline
Installable on mobile (PWA support)

Open the app link in your browser
